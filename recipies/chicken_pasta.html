Chicken pasta

A picutre of deliciuos Pasta!

Ingredients

  • 8 ounce package spaghettini
  • 5 tablespoons olive oil
  • 8 roma tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 tablespoon ketchup
  • 1 green bell pepper

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Heat 2 tablespoons oil
  3. Heat remaining 3 tablespoons
  4. Remove chicken form skillet and reserve
  5. Toss chicken and vegetables with tomato sauce and hot pasta
