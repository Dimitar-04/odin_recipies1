American goulash

A picutre of deliciuos goulash!

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • 1 large onion
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 2 large bay leaves
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Heat oil pot
  2. Cook, stirring occasionally
  3. Increase heat
  4. Remove from the heat and discard bay leaves
